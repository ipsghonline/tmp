## Migration Checklist

### Before Your Appointment

- [ ] Laptop and charger ready
- [ ] Stable internet connection available
- [ ] Mobile device charged (for Teams call)
- [ ] Critical local files backed up
- [ ] Migration time coordinated with manager

### During Migration (~2 hours)

- [ ] Join Teams support call from mobile device
- [ ] Follow technician instructions
- [ ] Do not interrupt device during wipe/enrollment
- [ ] Verify successful enrollment at ESP completion

### After Migration

- [ ] Wait until late Sunday for profile provisioning
- [ ] Verify email access in Outlook
- [ ] Test FloorSight and RFMS access
- [ ] Report any issues to IT support

---

## What You'll Lose Access To

After migration, you will **no longer** have access to:

- ILG environment
- Previous email configuration
- Old VPN profiles
- ILG SharePoint sites

---

## What You'll Gain Access To (Late Sunday)

- **RFMS** - Regional Floor Management System
- **Email** - Impact environment Outlook
- **FloorSight** - Flooring operations platform
- **OneDrive** - Your migrated files
- **Teams** - New Impact tenant
- **Microsoft 365** - Full suite
