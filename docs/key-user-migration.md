## Preparation Checklist

Please have the following ready for your appointment:

---

## What to Expect During Migration

The migration process will take **up to two hours**.

During the session:

- We will perform an **Intune device wipe** and re-enroll your device into the new Impact environment
- You can complete this from **any location** with a stable internet connection

**Important**: Once your device is migrated, you will **no longer be able to reconnect** to the ILG environment.

---

## Temporary Downtime

After migration, you will **not have access** to the following until late Sunday night:

- RFMS
- Email
- Other ILG systems

This timing ensures everything is aligned and ready for Monday morning support.

---

## Post-Migration Access (Late Sunday Night)

Starting late Sunday night, January 18, you will have access to:

- RFMS
- Email
- Locally installed applications
- FloorSight
- Other Impact-configured resources
